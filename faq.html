How are projects choosen for Volunteer Overseas ?

Volunteer Overseas partners with reputable organization worlwide that offer meaningfull volunteer,intern,

and teach abroad trips.We want to make sure that any program you sign up for is well-organized,safe,and

impactful.A few things we look for in our partner organization include:


-History and leadership

-Award,accreditation,and accolades

-Participant safety and support
